Acknowledgments

This book would not exist without the musicians I interviewed. I have never loved working with any material as much as I have loved working with their words. I hope more than anything that I’ve done their diverse perspectives justice. Any royalties I earn from this book will go directly to the Future of Music Coalition and CASH Music in the hopes of paying forward a little bit of the generosity you have shown me.
